About Me

I was born on February 10, 1998 in Gaithersburg, Maryland. After moving around the country for three years, my family decided to settle in a small town in California called Pleasanton. There, I began my gruesome academic carreer of learning my ABC's and 123's.

In the spring of 2016, I graduated from Amador Valley High School. Heavily influenced by my high school Computer Science teacher, I moved on to pursue an education in Computer Science at Texas A&M University. Eventually, I moved back to the Bay Area to continue expanding my knowledge of the field.
